Myself Jameskutty Thomas (Pet name: Raju) from the village of Perumpuzha, Kollam, Kerala, India. But, now I am settled in Vyttila, Ernakulam. Basically I am an Electrical Engineer from the 1987 batch of TKM College of Engineering, Kollam. My post graduation is in Opto-Electronics & Laser Technology from Cochin University of Science & Technology, Kochi. With 29 years experience as a professional Electrical Engineer, retired as Electrical Inspector from the Department of Electrical Inspectorate, Government of Kerala. I have qualified as Chartered Engineer (India). Now, my intention is to share the experiences procured with Electrical & Lightning Protection designs to the interested audience. Hence, I have launched a consulting firm named AJ Electrical & Lightning Protection Consultancy in Ernakulam, targetting Training, Consultancy, Auditing, Inspection, Valuation, etc.

About family, my wife is Anitha James and we are blessed with two children, boys; Daniel James and Joseph James.
